Based on what you've told us You could save money on NHS prescriptions

You can pay a set price for prescriptions, no matter how many you need.

A single prescription item costs £8.60. You can prepay for all your prescriptions for:

  • 3 months at £29.10
  • 12 months at £104

To spread the cost, you can prepay yearly with 10 monthly Direct Debit instalments of £10.40.

When it works out cheaper

It is cheaper to prepay if you buy either:

  • 4 or more prescriptions in 3 months
  • 13 or more prescriptions in a year
