Based on what you've told us You get full help with health costs

Because you get {{tctype}} and have a household income of £15,276 or less,

You get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental treatment
  • NHS sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You’ll also get some money off the cost of:

  • new glasses or contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing your glasses or contact lenses
  • travelling for NHS treatment

Your partner and any children under 20 included on your tax credit claim are also entitled to full help with health costs.

Proving you don't pay

A tax credit exemption certificate is a small plastic card which should have been sent to you. It lasts up to 7 months.

If you've lost it, you can use your latest tax credit award notice as proof.
