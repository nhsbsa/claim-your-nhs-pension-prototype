Based on what you've told us You get help with NHS costs

You get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental check-ups and treatment
  • NHS sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You also get some money off the cost of:

  • new glasses and contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing your glasses and contact lenses

    • You don't automatically get help towards the cost of:

    • travel for NHS treatment

    However, if your parents are on certain benefits or have little money to live off, you may still be able to get help to pay for travel.

    How to get your help

    Your date of birth is usually on your health records but you may have to show proof that you are in full time education, like a recent letter from school or college.

    To get free prescriptions

    Tick box B (second box down) on the back of the prescription form.
