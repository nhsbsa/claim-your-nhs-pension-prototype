How to get your help

To get free prescriptions

You need a valid exemption certificate.

Maternity exemption certificate

You can apply for a maternity exemption certificate while you're pregnant and up to 12 months after the birth of your baby.

Get an application form from your midwife, GP or health visitor.

Loss of a baby- Prescriptions

If you lost your baby after the 24th week of your pregnancy, you can still apply for a maternity exemption certificate.

If you have a miscarriage, abortion or stillborn birth you can use your maternity exemption certificate until it expires.

Medical exemption certificate

Speak to your GP about your medical condition, who can apply for you.
