Why are you claiming your deferred benefits for the 1995 Section?

This will normally be your 60th Birthday.

Early payment of deferred benefits are available to you at the age of:

  • 50 – 60 if you joined the scheme between 01/04/2000 and 05/04/2006 and you had a break in service of less than 5 years
  • 55 – 60 if joined the scheme on or after 06/04/2006
  • 55 - 60 if you had a break in service of 5 years or more, then re-joined the scheme on or after 06/04/2006

You can only choose this option if you have been approved by the Scheme’s medical advisers.
