Claim your NHS Pension

You are already entitled to a lump sum. Do you want to increase your lump sum by giving up part of your 1995 pension?

If you have 1995 Section membership, you’re already entitled to a lump sum. If your membership is from on or after 1 April 2008, you can choose to increase this lump sum.

How increasing my lump sum affects my benefits

For every £1 you give up from your pension, you’ll get £12 of your lump sum.

For example, if you give up £100 of your pension, you’d get £1200 of lump sum.
