Claim your NHS Pension

This service should take approximately 20 minutes to complete.

Important This form should not be submitted until 3 months before your expected retirement date.

You will need:

  • UK bank account details
  • details of any other pensions you have

You may also need:

  • date of marriage or civil partnership
  • spouse or civil partners date of birth and National Insurance number
  • dependant children's date of birth
  • an HMRC Protection Certificate if you have been issued one

Documents you will find helpful:

  • your recent estimate, if you have one
  • your maximum tax free lump sum, this can be found on your estimate
