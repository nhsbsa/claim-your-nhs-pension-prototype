You can add details for up to 6 dependant children. If you want to add any more dependant children, send us their details by post.

Who's classed as a dependant child

Where your Scheme membership extends to or beyond 1 April 2008, a dependant child is:

  • under age 23
  • aged 23 or over and unable to earn a living due to permanent physical or mental illness

Where your Scheme membership ended before 1 April 2008, a dependant child is:

  • under age 17
  • aged 17 or over, and have been in full-time education or training since the age of 17, up until the age of 23
  • aged 17 or over and unable to earn a living due to permanent physical or mental illness
