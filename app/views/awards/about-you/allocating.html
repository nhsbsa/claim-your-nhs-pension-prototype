What is allocation?

Allocation is where you permanently give up part of your pension now. If you die, whoever you have chosen will receive a pension. They have to be dependent on you when you retire and when you die.

You’ll have to pay for a medical and we’ll send you more information about this.

Surviving partner benefits are not affected if you choose to allocate.

How is this different to a death benefit nomination?

You can nominate someone to receive an adult dependant's pension and lump sum on death benefit if you die.

This does not affect the amount of pension you receive.
