Declaration

I confirm that:

  • I’ve read the Retirement Guide for members of the NHS Pension Scheme and the application guidance notes
  • the information I’ve given is correct and complete to the best of my knowledge
  • if I become aware of any changes in the information I’ve given after I‘ve sent it, I will notify NHS Pensions as soon possible
  • I’ll notify NHS Pensions if I recycle my NHS Pension Commencement Lump Sum
  • I understand that I’ll have to repay any overpayment of pension if necessary
  • if I have Enhanced, Primary, Fixed (2012 or 2014) or Individual Protection (2014) from HMRC I have enclosed a copy of the certificate
  • if I have Fixed or Individual Protection 2016 I have enclosed a copy of HMRC's notification, confirming the reference and / or certificate number
  • I understand that by providing a copy of HMRC's notification of 2016 Protection, I give permission for NHS Pensions to check and validate that protection with HMRC
  • if I have Fixed Protection 2012, 2014 or 2016, I have checked for Benefit Accrual and have not had Benefit Accrual up to and including the date of my retirement
  • I understand that, if I have claimed my 1995 Section benefits on normal age grounds or voluntary early retirement with actuarial reduction, my pension may be suspended if I return to NHS employment (including a Direction Body) within one calendar month. Work in the NHS which totals 16 hours or less a week is ignored for this purpose
  • I understand that if I claim my benefits from the 1995 Section, I will not be able to rejoin the Scheme
  • I’m entitled to apply for my retirement benefits from the NHS Pension Scheme
Agree and submit my application/
