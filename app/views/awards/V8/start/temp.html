Claim your NHS Pension

This service is for deferred members and should take approximately 20 minutes to complete.

Important

You cannot use this online service if you have HM Revenue & Customs Lifetime Allowance protection. You will need to Download and complete the paper form.


Before you start your claim, read the guidance notes and our fair processing notice.

To make your claim, you will need:

  • your UK bank account details
  • details of any other pensions you have

If they apply to you, you will also need:

  • the date of your marriage or civil partnership
  • the date of birth and National Insurance number of your spouse or civil partner
  • the date of birth for any dependant child
  • details of any other pensions

You will find the following documents helpful if you have them:

  • your recent estimate, if you have one
  • a printer to print confirmation of your application

This form should not be submitted until 3 months before your expected retirement date.
