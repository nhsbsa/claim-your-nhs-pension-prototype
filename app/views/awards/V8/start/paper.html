We cannot accept your NHS Pension claim online

Warning

You must use a paper form

What to do next

{#

You will need to apply for your NHS pension using the paper form.

#}

You must:

  • download and complete the paper application form
  • send the paper certificate(s) or a copy of HMRC's notification with the paper application form
  • the certificate must confirm the 2016 protection reference number

If we do not recieve the application and the certificate together your benefits may be delayed.

Your pensions office will send a copy to HMRC.
