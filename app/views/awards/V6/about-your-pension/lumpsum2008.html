Claim your NHS Pension

Do you want to give up part of your 2008 Section pension to receive a lump sum?

This is in addition to your mandatory lump sum equivalent to your entitlement under the 1995 Section. It applies for membership up to 31 March 2008, if you chose to move from the 1995 Section to the 2008 Section.

How increasing my lump sum affects my benefits

For every £1 of pension exchanged you’ll receive £12 of lump sum.

For example, if you exchange £100 of your pension, you’d get £1200 of lump sum.
