Claim your NHS Pension

What is your spouse's or civil partner's legally recognised gender?

We need to know this to calculate your pension entitlement, as a small number of gender specific regulations remain in the NHS Pension Scheme.

How legal gender is identified

A person’s legal gender is tied to their UK birth certificate unless they apply for a Gender Recognition Certificate (GRC) to change it legally. We have more guidance on legally recognised gender available.
