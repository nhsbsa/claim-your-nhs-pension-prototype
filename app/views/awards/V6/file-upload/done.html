What you need to do next

Send us your:

  • birth certificate
  • marriage certificate
  • HMRC Lifetime Allowance protection certificate
  • return address

You must send these to:

NHS Pensions PO Box 2269 Bolton BL6 9JS

What we'll do next

If you send us any certificates, we'll send them back to you in the same way we received them. For example, recorded delivery.

Your pension will be processed within either:

  • 30 calendar days from date you retired
  • 30 days from the date NHS Pensions receives all the information required to calculate and pay your benefits

When we process your application, we'll contact you if we need any further information from you and let you know whether this will cause a delay.
